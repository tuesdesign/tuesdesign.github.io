TAGS

AUTOMATE EVERYTHING.


TUESDAY TAGS are NFC-based devices that help you control your smart devices. TUESDAY TAGS are configurable and reconfigurable, so you can use them to automate many different actions. Have one toggle the lights when you get in bed, make coffee when you wake up, order more of a product online when you run out, or email you a random picture of a cat. Compatible with IFTTT and apps like Tasker.
